TriggeredWebJobOperations

instance of the [WebSiteExtensionsClient] {@link
WebSiteExtensionsClient~WebSiteExtensionsClient}.

WebSiteExtensionsClient~WebSiteExtensionsClient#triggeredWebJobs}.
